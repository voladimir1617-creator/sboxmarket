Cookie Policy

Last updated: 14 April 2026.

SkinBox uses the smallest possible set of cookies — only what the Service technically cannot run without. There is no advertising, no retargeting, no Google Analytics, and no cross-site tracking.

Cookies we set

Name Purpose Expires Type
JSESSIONID Authenticates your browser to the backend after you log in via Steam OpenID. Browser session Essential
XSRF-TOKEN CSRF double-submit token protecting state-changing requests. Browser session Essential
sb_currency Remembers your preferred display currency (USD / EUR / GBP / …). 1 year Preference
sb_theme Remembers your accent color pick from the theme picker. 1 year Preference

What we do NOT use

  • No Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Segment, or Amplitude.
  • No Facebook Pixel, TikTok Pixel, or Twitter/X tracking.
  • No ad network cookies.
  • No third-party retargeting or remarketing cookies.
  • No fingerprinting libraries.

How to disable cookies

Every modern browser lets you block or clear cookies per site. Doing that on SkinBox means you can't log in — the session cookie is essential — but you can still browse listings as a guest.
