Refund Policy

Last updated: 14 April 2026.
Default: all sales final. Digital goods delivered through Steam are non-returnable once the trade offer has been accepted. Please read the full policy below before you buy.

1. When you get a refund

SkinBox will issue a full refund in the following narrow set of cases:

  • Double-charge: Stripe captured the same payment twice due to a retry bug. We refund the duplicate within 5 business days of detection.
  • Trade offer never sent: You paid, but our trade bot failed to send you a Steam offer within 24 hours. Contact support with your order ID.
  • Wrong item delivered: The item you received is demonstrably different from the one you paid for (e.g. different skin, different float, different condition). You must report within 7 days, and we must be able to verify via Steam trade history.
  • Platform outage: SkinBox charged you but we were unable to match your order to a listing because of a confirmed outage on our side.

2. When you do NOT get a refund

  • The item's price dropped after you bought it.
  • You changed your mind.
  • You accidentally bought the wrong item.
  • You got scammed by another user off-platform (e.g. a side-channel DM on Discord).
  • Facepunch removed or reworked the item after purchase.
  • Your Steam account was trade-locked, 2FA-locked, or banned after purchase.
  • You rejected the trade offer and the hold expired.
  • You waited more than 7 days to report a problem.

3. Wallet withdrawals

Withdrawals from your SkinBox wallet to a Stripe-verified bank account are processed within 1–3 business days. A 1.5% platform fee applies. Once the Stripe payout status is "paid", the transaction is irreversible.

4. Chargebacks

Issuing a chargeback against SkinBox without first contacting support will result in immediate account suspension and forfeiture of any wallet balance. We will also submit a full dispute response to your card issuer including the IP address you logged in from, the Steam trade history, and the Stripe radar score. Fraudulent chargebacks may be reported to law enforcement.

5. Refund process

Email support@skinbox.example with your order ID, the reason for the refund request, and any supporting screenshots. We respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds post to your original payment method within 5–10 business days depending on your bank.
