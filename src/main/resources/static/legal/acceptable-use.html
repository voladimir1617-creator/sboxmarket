Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: 14 April 2026.

SkinBox exists to let s&box players trade cosmetic items with less friction than the Steam Community Market. Anything that undermines that goal — or hurts other users — is not allowed. Violations can result in listing removal, account suspension, permanent ban, wallet forfeiture, and in some cases referral to law enforcement.

Zero-tolerance violations

  • Listing items you do not own or have no authority to sell.
  • Selling items obtained via account theft, phishing, API key theft, or any other form of unauthorized access.
  • Using SkinBox to launder funds, evade sanctions, or convert stolen crypto into cash.
  • Selling items that depict or glorify illegal activity, sexual content involving minors, or credible threats of violence.
  • Harassing other users, SkinBox staff, or our support team.
  • Attempting to bypass a ban by creating a new account.

Market integrity violations

  • Wash trading (buying your own listings to inflate volume or price).
  • Coordinated price manipulation with other users.
  • Listing the same item on SkinBox and elsewhere, then cancelling the SkinBox listing after a buyer locks it.
  • Creating "ghost" listings you have no intention of filling.
  • Misrepresenting item quality, float, pattern, or collection.

Technical violations

  • Scraping SkinBox at a rate above the rate-limit, or circumventing the rate-limit.
  • Reverse-engineering our API or extension to find unpublished endpoints.
  • Uploading malware, exploits, or code that targets other users.
  • Probing the site for security vulnerabilities without permission. If you find one, report it to security@skinbox.example.
  • Running automated bidding bots on auctions.

Communication violations

  • Spamming chat, reviews, or the marketplace listing descriptions.
  • Posting phishing links or fake support addresses.
  • Impersonating SkinBox staff, other users, or Facepunch employees.
  • Leaking private information about other users ("doxing").

Consequences

  • First technical violation: warning + listing removal.
  • First market-integrity violation: 7-day suspension + affected listings reversed.
  • Zero-tolerance violation: permanent ban + wallet freeze + potential legal action.

Appeals: appeals@skinbox.example. We will review but are not obligated to reinstate an account.
